

# FightSoul – Conversion-Optimized Homepage

A bold, dark-themed single-page homepage in German designed to drive free trial bookings for FightSoul Kampfsportstudio in Thun.

---

## 1. Hero Section
- Full-width dark background with a striking boxing/training image overlay (dimmed)
- FightSoul logo top-left, sticky navigation top-right (Angebot, Team, Kontakt)
- Phone number + address always visible in nav bar
- Large bold headline: **"Dein Kampfsportstudio für echte Resultate in Thun"**
- Short 1-line subtext about fitness, Kampfsport & personal development
- Prominent red **"Jetzt kostenlos Probieren!"** CTA button (links to SportsNow)
- Subtle scroll-down indicator arrow

## 2. Social Proof Bar
- Horizontal strip showing key stats: e.g. "100+ aktive Mitglieder", "5⭐ Google Bewertungen", "Erfahrene J+S Trainer"
- Clean icons with numbers, builds instant trust

## 3. Angebote (Services) Section
- Section heading: "Unsere Angebote"
- 6 cards in a responsive grid (2x3 desktop, 1 column mobile):
  - Kampfsport
  - J+S Kickboxing Light (6-16 Jahre)
  - Personaltraining
  - Functional Training / HIIT
  - Onlinecoaching
  - Integralcoaching (Coming Soon badge)
- Each card: icon, title, short description, "Mehr erfahren" link
- Red accent on hover

## 4. Why FightSoul Section
- 3 benefit columns with icons:
  - "Dein Erfolg im Fokus" – professional coaching
  - "Gemeinschaft und Motivation" – supportive community
  - "Flexibilität" – for every age and fitness level
- Dark card styling with subtle red accent borders

## 5. Testimonials / Trust Section
- 2-3 testimonial quotes in a carousel or cards
- Placeholder content (to be replaced with real reviews)
- Star ratings displayed prominently

## 6. CTA Banner
- Bold mid-page conversion section with dark/red gradient
- "Starte jetzt – dein Erfolg wartet!"
- Large red CTA button repeating the free trial booking link
- Creates urgency and captures scrollers

## 7. Team Preview
- Section showing 2-3 trainer photos with name and specialty
- Builds personal connection and trust
- Placeholder images with slots for real photos

## 8. Location & Contact Footer
- Address: Seestrasse 14, 3600 Thun
- Phone: +41 (0) 79 935 53 03
- Embedded map placeholder area
- Social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, etc.)
- Final CTA button for free trial

## Design Details
- **Color palette**: Dark background (#1a1a2e or similar), red accents (#e63946), white/light text
- **Typography**: Bold, modern sans-serif headings; clean body text
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in on scroll for sections
- **Mobile-first**: Fully responsive, hamburger menu on mobile
- **All CTAs** link to the SportsNow booking page
